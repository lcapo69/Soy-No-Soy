Soy No Soy

Home

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 3 tablespoons flour
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 1 can condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 16 ounce package egg noodles
  • 1/2 cup sour cream

Instructions

In a large pan, brown the meat. Drain if necessary (see note).

Add flour and stir to coat the beef.

Add soup, broth, and garlic powder to the meat and stir to combine. Bring to a simmer over medium heat.

Add egg noodles and stir to cover them as much as possible in the liquid.

Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 12 minutes or until the noodles are cooked through.

Stir in sour cream and serve warm.
