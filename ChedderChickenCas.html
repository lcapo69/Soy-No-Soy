Ingredients

  • Cream of chicken soup
  • 9 turns of the sea salt
  • 20 turns of the pepper
  • 1 and 3/4 cup of rice
  • Beef bouillon and 1.5 cups of water
  • 1/2 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1 full bag of broccoli florets ONLY

Instructions

Mix all ingredients together really well.

Take water out or pour more in if needed. Make sure broccoli is covered with at least a little water to keep it damp.

Top the casserole with another 1/2 cup or 1/4 cup of shredded cheddar cheese.

Cover the dish with tin foil and place in the oven at 375Â°F.

Bake for 50 minutes. After 50 minutes, remove the foil and bake uncovered for another 8 minutes.

Done! Mush up the broccoli on your plate for that nostalgic, blended flavor.
