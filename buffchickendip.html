Buffalo chicken dip

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 package (8 ounces) cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup Frank's RedHotÂ® Original Cayenne Pepper Hot Sauce
  • 1/2 cup ranch dressing
  • 1/2 cup cheddar cheese + extra for topping

Instructions

Combine all ingredients in slow cooker

Cook in slow cooker on high for 2 hours

Shread chicken in slow cooker, then cook on low for 4 hours or on high for 2 hour.

Add layer of chedder cheese to the top, serve when melted.
