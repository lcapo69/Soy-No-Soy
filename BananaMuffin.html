Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups (385 g) well-mashed ripe bananas (about 3 large bananas)
  • ¼ cup (60 ml) avocado oil or vegetable/canola oil
  • ¼ cup (60 g) unsalted butter, melted and cooled
  • ½ cup (100 g) sugar
  • ½ cup (100 g) light brown sugar, tightly packed
  • 2 large eggs, room temperature preferred
  • ¼ cup (60 ml) buttermilk, room temperature preferred
  • 1 Tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups (250 g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt

Streusel (Optional)

  • ½ cup (65 g) all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup (100 g) brown sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ cup (60 g) unsalted butter, cold

Instructions

Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a muffin tin with paper liners.

Place peeled bananas in a large bowl and mash them well using a fork or potato masher.

Add oil and melted butter and stir well.

Stir in sugars until well combined.

Add eggs, buttermilk, and vanilla extract and stir again. Set aside.

In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.

Add the dry mixture to your wet batter and gently fold in until just combined. Don’t overmix!

Portion batter into the muffin tin, filling each cup ¾ full. Set aside while preparing the streusel.

Streusel Topping (Optional)

In a medium bowl, stir together flour, brown sugar, and salt.

Use a pastry cutter to cut in cold butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

Sprinkle streusel evenly over each muffin.

Bake muffins at 425°F (220°C) for 8 minutes, then reduce oven to 350°F (175°C) without opening the door. Bake another 7–8 minutes.

A toothpick in the center should come out clean or with a few moist crumbs.

Let muffins cool before enjoying.
