NY Style Pizza

Ingredients

  • Filtered water room temp(650g)
  • All purpose flour (1000g)
  • Yeast (4g)
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Fine sea salt (20g)

Instructions

Add water to mixing bowl

Add yeast to mixing bowl with water

Add salt to mixing bowl

Mix it all to gether till the dough pulls from the side of the bowl. (can use dough hook or wooden spoon)

When there is not longer any dry flour in the bowl, add olive oil. Then mix for an additional 20 minutes

Hand mix dough for a few seconds, then cover it and let it rest at room tempurature for 1 hour.

Place dough on cutting board. Cut dough into balls roughly 250 grams in weight.

Stretch dough balls by folding them in on themselves (see original for demo). Pinch bottom of the ball to seal it up.

Place dough balls in a lightly greased container and seal the container.

Put containers in the fridge, and let the dough balls proof for 24-48 hours.

Pull dough balls out 30-60 mintues before use to allow the dough balls to rest at room temp.

Remove dough from container and plop on a floured surface to stretch.

Add toppingss

Bake on pizza stone or steel at 500-550 for 7-10 min
